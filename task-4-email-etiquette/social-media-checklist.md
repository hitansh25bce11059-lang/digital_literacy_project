# 📱 Social Media Safety: Do’s & Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ DO’s (Safe Practices)

- Use **strong, unique passwords** for each account  
- Enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)**  
- Check **privacy settings** regularly  
- Only accept requests from **people you know**  
- Think before posting — *"Would I be okay if everyone saw this?"*  
- Verify links and messages before clicking  
- Log out from shared or public devices  
- Report suspicious accounts or messages  

---

## ❌ DON’Ts (Risky Behaviors)

- Don’t share **passwords** with anyone (even friends)  
- Don’t post **personal information** (address, phone number, school, etc.)  
- Don’t click on **unknown or suspicious links**  
- Don’t trust messages that create **urgency or fear** (e.g., "Act now!")  
- Don’t accept requests from **strangers**  
- Don’t overshare your **location in real-time**  
- Don’t engage with **bullying or harmful content**  
- Don’t ignore warning signs — report or block immediately  

---

## ⚠️ Quick Reality Check

> If it feels **too good to be true, urgent, or suspicious — it probably is.**

---

## 🎯 Smart Rule to Remember

> **Pause → Think → Verify → Act**
